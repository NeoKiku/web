About Neo Kiku Studios

Neo Kiku Studios explores the intersection of technology and humanity through cyberpunk storytelling. Our name combines "Neo" (new, futuristic) with "Kiku" (chrysanthemum in Japanese), representing the balance between digital innovation and organic beauty.

We craft narratives that examine what makes us human in an increasingly digital world, finding hope and beauty in the spaces between tradition and tomorrow.
